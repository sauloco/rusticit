Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using our Chrome Extension, Refined JW. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of anonymous data to improve our service:

  • User Action: Specific actions taken within the extension.
  • User ID: An anonymized identifier for distinguishing users.
  • Recurrent User Status: Information on whether the user is a recurrent user.
  • Location: Determines whether the action is related to 'wol' or 'jw'.
  • Screen Information: Screen width and height.
  • User Agent: Information about the user's browser and device.
  • Language Information: The preferred language and additional languages supported by the browser.
  • Platform Information: Information about the user's operating system platform.
  • Touch Points: Number of maximum touch points supported by the device.
  • Timestamp: The time at which the data was collected.

How We Use the Information

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving Service: To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality and performance of the extension.
  • User Experience: To provide a more personalized experience based on usage data.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any collected data to third parties. All information is used internally for the purposes outlined above.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

User Rights

As our data collection is fully anonymous, we do not store any personally identifiable information. However, users have the right to contact us if they have concerns about their data usage.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us on the official issues page
